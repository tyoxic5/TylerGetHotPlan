RECIPES
Base — Bulk Shredded Chicken

Ingredients: 3-3.5 lbs boneless chicken breast, 1 cup chicken broth, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, salt & pepper.

Method: Slow cooker, low 6-7 hrs or high 3-4 hrs. Shred with two forks right in the pot. Makes ~10 servings — this is the base for both meals below.

Black Bean Bowl (Meal A)

Per batch (5): 5 portions bulk chicken (6oz), 1 cup salsa, 2.5 cups dry rice, 15oz can black beans.

Method: Warm chicken with a few spoonfuls of salsa until coated. Serve over rice with black beans.

Lemon Broccoli Chicken (Meal B)

Per batch (5): 5 portions bulk chicken (7oz), juice of 2-3 lemons, 2 cloves garlic, 1 tbsp olive oil, 1.5 lbs broccoli, 2.5 cups dry rice.

Method: Warm chicken with lemon juice, garlic, and olive oil. Steam broccoli. Serve together over rice.
